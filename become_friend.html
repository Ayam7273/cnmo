What is a Friend of CNMO?

A "Friend of CNMO" is an individual supporter who believes in our mission to unite Nigerian Muslims across the UK, promote Islamic values, and serve the wider community. As a Friend, you stand with us in spirit and in action - receiving our updates, attending our events, and helping us spread positivity through Islam.

You do not need to be a Nigerian or belong to a Muslim organisation to be a Friend of CNMO. Anyone who shares our values of unity, compassion, and service to humanity is welcome to join.

Earn Reward
