Why Volunteer?

Volunteering with CNMO is an opportunity to serve your community while earning the reward of Allah (SWT). Our volunteers are the backbone of everything we do - from organising events and supporting projects to creating content, assisting with coordination, and welcoming community members.

Whether you have a few hours a month or can commit more regularly, there is always a role for you. CNMO welcomes volunteers of all backgrounds, skills, and ages who share our commitment to unity, Islamic values, and community service.

Events Support

Help organise and run our lectures, conferences, and community gatherings across the UK.

Digital & Media

Assist with social media, graphic design, video production, or website content creation.

Welfare & Outreach

Support community welfare initiatives and help connect members with available resources and services.
